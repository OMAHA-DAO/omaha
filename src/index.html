Fundamentals

Omaha DAOs voting system is based on Vitalik Butarin’s quadratic voting mechanism. It aims to become the fairest and most transparent voting system in web3. Its intuitive user experience paves the way to mass-adoption and creates a long-term, engaging way of managing a multi-million investment fund.

Community Governance is the core value of the community-outlined Omaha Codex. Every single investor participates in the fund management with their vote, not with their wealth. Capped voting power means collective responsibility for the investment of every individual.

Fund Strategists are an integral driving force of Omaha DAO. They analyze and suggest trading options to the community who finally executes the trades. All suggestions are evaluated in an AI-based performance ranking. Competition among Fund Strategists steadily increases the swarm intelligence of Omaha DAO.

Omaha DAO invests in stocks and crypto assets to build its Treasury. The Proof of Concept will be conducted by buying Synthetic Stocks via Mirror Protocol. Following this, the portfolio will be extended to real-world financial assets, consequently becoming the world’s first community-driven investment fund.

$OMAHA token is a multifunctional financial instrument backed by real life assets. Tokenomics have built-in Anti-Inflation Reflux and a Token Burn Mechanism. A protected Floor Price and limited Maximum Supply provide the prerequisites for a steady and sustainable increase of the intrinsic token value.

The community of Omaha DAO may redeem the fund treasury at any chosen moment by vote. Cash out will take the form of treasury assets proportional to the token holdings of every single investor. A collateralized floor price of $OMAHA saves investors from emotional downtrends and supports market stability.
